About Me

I am a Ph.D. graduate in Economics from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras and presently working as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Economic Sciences, IISER Bhopal. My areas of specialization are Microeconomics, Applied Econometrics, Industrial Economics and Efficiency and Productivity Analysis. My research interests lie in applied economic analysis, particularly the performance evaluation using Data Envelopment Analysis(DEA) and Bootstrap DEA approach. I am also interested in studies using primary survey data.
